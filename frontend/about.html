About Us

INREM IN INDIA

INREM works across India in 7 states directly and with partners in most placs reacheong out on environ mental problems such as water pollution affecting millions of people in India.
We have the main coordination team based in Anand(Gujarat) and locational teams in other places

THE ANAND TEAM

INREM's Anand team plays a strategic role for the organization and for wider stakeholders engaging on key issues such as Water quality and health. It is a 10 person team composed of a mix of planners, communication specialists and technical experts in the areas that INREM works.

SAFE WATER FOR BALASORE

This coastal district of eastern Odisha has its own set of ecological concerns, among them being Water quality issues.
Our Balasore team works closely with multiple civil society and government partners on problems such as Fluorosis mitigation.

FINALLY, HOPE IN NALGONDA

Nalgonda, in Telangana, is
synonymous with "Fluorosis" an almost
hundred year old disease from contaminated
water.
Disability and crippling is all now being addressed with Safe water , nutrition and a convergence approach.

SUSTAINING WATER IN CHIKBALLAPUR

Extreme water scarcity signified by thousands of dry deep borewells and a high fluoride problem in groundwater combine together to create a difficult water management problem in this district of Karnataka, in southern India. Our team here brings in a variety of local and regional groups to work on solutions for the problems here.

MAKING WATER SOLUTIONS WORK IN NALBARI

Assam gives an impression of the Brahmaputra river and water in plenty. But little do we know that beneath this plentiness, lies a story of water contamination - Arsenic, fluoride and other toxins in water.
Our teams helps sustain local and ecological solutions, alongwith helping control further spread of the diseases here.

FLOODS AND WATER CONTAMINATION IN BUXAR

A double whammy of repeated floods and a cancer hotspot puts Buxar in Bihar, eastern India, into a cycle of poverty.
Arsenic in water is one reason behind this. Our team here works to detect Arsenicosis at early stages and helps sustain community based solutions to the Arsenic problem.

MAKING EVERY DROP OF RAIN COUNT IN DUNGARPUR

Dungarpur in southern Rajasthan, is an undulating area with tribal pockets and high water scarcity. High fluoride in water brings another layer of trouble for communities here. With a combination of strong community based action, and conserving Rainwater, our team here, has brought much hope to the people of this district

HEALING CHILDREN FROM DISABILITY

Our oldest field programme in Jhabua, Madhya Pradesh, pioneered the approach of Safe drinking water and good nutrition to tackle fluorosis. Healing children from a life of disability is a mission for our team here.
Neighbouring Ali Rajpur is also a district where fresher effort is being made.

BUILDING COMMUNITY RESILIENCE TO WATER IN BHAGALPUR

High Arsenic in water is making life difficult for thousands of people living in the Gangetic plains of Bhagalpur in Bihar. Floods make this problem worse. By early stage detection of the problem, and healing with Safe water, our team helps to bring relief to the Arsenicosis affected people of Bhagalpur.
