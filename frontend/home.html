INREM FOUNDATION

INREM Foundation is a research institution probing societal issues concerning water, public health, agriculture and the environment. The institution develops innovative inter-disciplinary solutions and brings them into the wider domain of practice by participating with communities and government.INREM has teams in 7 states of India and anchors the Water Quality Network offering courses such as on Water Quality Management. The organization is a national Sector Partner of the Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) in India. INREM promotes the innovation initiative WaterHealthAI and developing thinking on Water Quality with Climate Change.The organization is supported by its partners such as the European Union, Arghyam, UNICEF, APPI and other agencies
